# Privacy Policy

**glp-ram** does not — and will never — collect, process, transmit, sell, or
store any user data.

The extension runs entirely on your own device. It manages your browser tabs
(unloading inactive ones to free memory, deferring background tabs, and relaying
your own pages' notifications) using only local browser APIs. It does not read,
record, or send the contents of any page you visit.

- **No data collection.** No browsing history, page content, URLs, personal
  information, or analytics are gathered.
- **No external servers.** The extension makes no network requests of its own and
  contains no remote code, tracking, or third-party services.
- **Local-only storage.** Your settings and the extension's per-tab bookkeeping
  are kept only in your browser's own storage (`storage.sync` / `storage.session`)
  so the extension can function across restarts. This never leaves your device
  except via your own Chrome account sync, which is controlled entirely by you.

If this policy ever changes, this document will be updated before any such change
takes effect.
